Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13th March 2026

At Drain Unblocking Dublin, your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how we protect and manage your personal information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We firmly believe in data minimization. We do not automatically harvest or collect personal data from you simply for visiting our site.

We only hold information that you actively choose to share with us. This usually happens when you fill out our contact form, request a call-out, or email us. The details we collect may include your name, email address, phone number, property address, and specific details about your drainage or plumbing issues.

2. Cookies and Tracking

We respect your digital footprint. This website does not use cookies, pixels, or third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or Meta Pixel). We do not monitor or record your browsing behavior.

3. How We Use Your Information

If you reach out to us, we use your details strictly to help resolve your issues. This includes:

  • Responding to your direct questions and inquiries.
  • Providing accurate estimates or quotes for drain unblocking and repairs.
  • Scheduling call-outs and dispatching our team to your property.
  • Communicating with you regarding the progress of your job.

4. Data Sharing

Your information stays with us. We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal details to third parties or marketing agencies.
